System Context

TransformPV acts as bridge between the Safety system and the organization gateway. The R2's generated by safety solution are transformed into R3 as per agency specific profiles i.e. FDA VAERS, EMA, eMDR. The transformed file is validated against the XSD before being transmitted to agencies via the organization gateway. The solution also performs the downgrade transformation i.e. R3 -> R2, the R3 messages and ACK received from agency/partner are transformed to R2 and are dropped to incoming R2 folders which are processed by safety system. TransformPV also provides an configurable option to make the source R3 XML and/or R2-R3 decode view available in Safety case form.
